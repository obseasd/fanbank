Three moves. That's the app.

Everything below signs from your own wallet. No custodian holds your USDt, no server sees your seed. Judges and users can audit every tx from the journal at the bottom of the page.

01

Tip a team or a player

Pick a national team from the picker, choose an amount, one signed USDt transfer goes straight to their tip address. No intermediary, no bridge.

02

Pool with your section

Open a shared pool ("Team France Berlin trip"). Fans contribute in USDt, the split policy (equal, proportional, winner-takes) is enforced at payout. Auditable end to end.

03

Bet the match

Parimutuel markets on knockout fixtures. Every stake is a real USDt transfer, odds recompute live from the pool, winners split at the final whistle with a 2% platform fee.

Play

Send a tip. Rally a pool.
