Three moves. That's the app.

Everything below signs from your own wallet. No custodian holds your USDt, no server sees your seed. Judges and users can audit every transaction from the journal at the bottom of the page.

01

Tip a team or a player

Pick a national team from the picker, choose an amount, one signed USDt transfer goes straight to their on-chain tip address. No intermediary, no bridge.

02

Pool with your section

Open a shared pool ("Team France Berlin trip"). Fans contribute in USDt, the split policy (equal, proportional, winner-takes) is enforced at payout. Auditable end to end.

03

Bet the match

Parimutuel markets on knockout fixtures. Every stake is a real USDt transfer, odds recompute live from the pool, winners split at the final whistle with a 2% platform fee.

Predict

Bet on match outcomes.

For every upcoming match, three outcomes: home wins, draw, or away wins. Pick your side, choose your USDt stake, and the transfer signs from your wallet. When the match ends, winners split the pool proportionally to their stake, minus a 2% platform fee. Odds you see are live: your stake divided by the winning side's total.

Compose

Tournament bracket, all-or-nothing knockout.

Fans lock a USDt stake on the team they back before the tournament starts. Each match, the loser's pool is redistributed to the winner's bettors proportional to their stake share. Your stake stays at risk until your team is eliminated or lifts the trophy. Bet on the champion for the full multiplier, or on a mid-tier team who wins a match or two and break even without the trophy.
